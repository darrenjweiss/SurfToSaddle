About Surf To Saddle

Our Origin: Where the Wild West Meets the Wild Coast

Surf To Saddle was born in the charming Victorian village of Ferndale, California, nestled perfectly where the ancient redwood forests meet the wild, untamed Pacific Ocean. Our brand is a tribute to this unique intersection of landscapes and lifestyles. We curate and create items that embody the spirit of the Pacific Northwest, reflecting both the rugged outdoor environment and the independent spirit of our community.
